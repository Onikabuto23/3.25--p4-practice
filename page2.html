The life of Zara Larsson

Early Life 1997-2010

Dec.16, 1997

----------Zara Larsson is born in Stockholm, Sweden

2008

----------Zara Larsson wins Talang(Swedens version of Got Talent)

----------She won Talang singing "My Heart Will Go On", which was orginally sung by Celine Dion

2009-10

----------Zara joins the Swedish reality show Jag ska bli stjärna, which follows young Swedish talents as they travel on the journey to hopeful international fame

Early breakout 2012-2017

2012-2013

----------Zara signs with TEN Music Group in 2012

----------Zara releases her first EP, titled "Introducing" 2013, with the title track "Uncover", topping the Sverigetopplistan charts

----------On the third of April 2013, Zara signs with Epic Records

----------On October first 2014 Zara releases her first studio album, named "1"

2015-17

----------Zara releases the single "Lush Life" in 2015 ahead of her 2nd studio album "So good"

----------Zara releases her second studio album "So Good" in 2017 to critical acclaim and commerical succes

Khia asylum 2018-early 2025

----------Zara remains in the Khia asylum for around 7 years

----------These years are caused mostly by Covid and label disputes

----------Even during thid time Zara releases 2 albums, named "Poster girl" and "Venus" Both recieved with critical acclaim, however low commercial succes

----------In late 2024-early 2025, Zara inched closer to the exit of the Khia asylum with the Dolphin trend to her song "Symphony"

Escape from the Khia asylum 2025-present

----------In mid early 2025 Zara Larsson is announced as the opener for the America leg of Tate Mcrae's Miss Possesive world tour

----------In April Zara releases the first single named "Pretty Ugly" from her next album "Midnight Sun

----------On June 13 2025 Zara released the second single named "Midnight Sun", which led to her first Grammy nomination for best Dance Pop recording

----------On September 26. Zara releases the album "Midnight Sun", to critical acclaim

----------On October 10. Pink Panthress released her remix album titled "Fancy Some More", which featured a remix of Pink's song "Stateside", this time featuring Zara Larsson

----------During the 2026 Winter Olympics after Alysa Liu performed to the "Stateside" remix, the song shot up in popularity, being the number one song on the Spotify Global charts, and reaching #6 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart
